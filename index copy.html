Kyle Nakagawa About Me

I play Clarinet, Bass Clarinet, Tenor Saxaprhone, and Piano. I enjoy playing with the Punahou Marching Band, Punahou Wind Ensemble, and the Punahou Jazz Band.
I also enjoy outdoor activities such as camping, hiking, and fishing with my boy scout friends.
I am from Kaneohe, Hawaii on the windward side of Oahu.
